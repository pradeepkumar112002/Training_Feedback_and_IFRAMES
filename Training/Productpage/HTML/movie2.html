Avengers: Endgame is a 2019 superhero film based on the Marvel Comics superhero team the Avengers. It is the 22nd film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) and serves as the conclusion to a story arc that began with the first Iron Man film in 2008. The film is directed by Anthony and Joe Russo and stars an ensemble cast including Robert Downey Jr. as Iron Man, Chris Evans as Captain America, Chris Hemsworth as Thor, Scarlett Johansson as Black Widow, Jeremy Renner as Hawkeye, Mark Ruffalo as the Hulk, and many others. In the film, the surviving members of the Avengers and their allies work to reverse the damage caused by Thanos in "Avengers: Infinity War". The film culminates in a showdown between the heroes and Thanos, leading to a conclusion that has lasting ramifications for the MCU. Avengers: Endgame was a critical and commercial success, breaking numerous box office records and receiving praise for its balancing of humor, drama, and action, as well as its ability to bring the story arcs of multiple characters to a satisfying conclusion.
